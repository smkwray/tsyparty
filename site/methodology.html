Methodology & Sources

Data provenance, pipeline architecture, and claims discipline

All counterparty inference outputs represent likely net counterparties inferred from public quarterly sector-level holding changes. They are not bilateral trade records, not exact counterparties, and not derived from proprietary transaction data. A residual or unexplained bucket is always preserved and displayed.

The canonical estimation frequency is quarterly. Primary-market allocation (auction buyer composition) and secondary-market reallocation (RAS-inferred sector flows) are modeled as separate objects. Households and residual categories are not literal transaction traces — they absorb classification noise and missing detail.
